Enrollment

If you plan to have you child to take piano lessons from me you will need to have the following materials

  • a keyboard or piano that is accesible every day
  • a set of note flash cards
  • The folowing Alfred's Premier Piano Course Books
    1. Performance 1A
    2. Lesson 1A
    3. Theory 1A
    4. Technique 1A
  • Metronome
  • A good twenty minute chunk of time
